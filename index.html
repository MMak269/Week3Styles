An Easy Recipe For Tiramisu

Below you will find a quick and easy recipe for Tiramisu


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups espresso or strong coffee, room temperature
  • 6 Tbsp golden rum, divided
  • 40 ladyfinger cookies
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 16 oz mascarpone, cold
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2-3 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions

  1. Prepare coffee and let cool down.
  2. Add egg yolks and sugar to bowl and using an electric mixer, mix for 4-5 minutes. Then add marscapone and mix gently until fully combined.
  3. In a separate bowl add heavy whipping cream and mix with an electric mixer until you get stiff peaks in your whipped cream.
  4. Combine your marscapone mixture with your whipped cream.
  5. Now to begin assembling your tiramisu. Start by dipping your ladyfinger cookies in coffee and create a layer at the bottom of your serving dish.
  6. Add a layer of half of your marscapone cream. Alternate creating layers of coffee-dipped ladyfingers and the second half of your marscapone cream
  7. Dust your tiramisu with cocoa powder and let rest in your fridge for 4-5 hours.
  8. Enjoy!
